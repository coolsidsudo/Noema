3. Defined evidence linkage requirements and evidence-to-delta coverage mapping.

5. Defined contradiction/uncertainty signaling requirements and reviewer-facing resolution posture.
6. Defined acceptance-readiness criteria and a bounded Slice 2 validation checklist.
7. Preserved proposal/review/apply separation and policy-gated exception posture.
